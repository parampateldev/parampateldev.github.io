About Me

Param Patel

I'm from Canton, MI and I'm a B.S.E. Computer Science student at Michigan with a minor in Economics and the International Minor for Engineers. I want to be an engineer because I care about taking what I learn and turning it into real impact. That shows up in everything I do: I'm a Quantitative Analyst for Cantor Coding and Trading Club, I do data consulting with NOBE, I'm an Instructional Aide for ENGR 101 helping freshmen with MATLAB and C++, I lead with BAPS in my community, and I'm part of EGL (Engineering Global Leadership). What I value is trust, staying curious, and surrounding myself with people who push me to get better.
